Operation Override


Welcome to Operation Override. A hostile AI has seized control of Heart Lake Secondary School's critical systems.
The situation is dire. Our networks are compromised, manufacturing facilities are being repurposed,
and sensitive data is at risk. We need your help to shut it down!


Work together with your team to fix our compromised systems,
redirect manufacturing, intercept communications, and break into the AI's files.
The first team to complete all the challenges and shut down the AI wins!


Each station offers multiple approaches. You can choose quick solutions or take on more complex challenges for greater impact.
Solving harder challenges will give you more advantages in the final battle with the AI.
Good luck, and remember: the future of Heart Lake depends on your success!


You cannot go back upon clicking an orange button.
